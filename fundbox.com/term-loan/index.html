How it works

  • Apply in two easy steps

    Let us assess the health of your business to get you a fast credit decision. First, share some basic information about your business. Then, connect your accounting software and business checking account, or just your checking account by itself. Applying is free and will not affect your credit score.

  • Choose your terms

    If approved, choose an amount to borrow, up to your approved limit, and your repayment plan, and review the fees up front. Offer is valid for 30 days.

  • Funds transfer right away

    When you take a term loan, the funds transfer to your bank account as soon as the next business day.

  • Pay us back over time

    Repay in equal installments over the course of your 24 or 52 week plan. Automatic payments make it easy. No prepayment penalty if you repay early.

Term loan guide

How could your business benefit from a term loan?

By providing you with a lump sum of cash that you can pay off over a set period of time, term loans can be a convenient way to get the funds you need for a specific larger purchase. Typically used to finance vehicles, machinery, or even real estate, term loans can also help to fund business ventures or buy inventory in bulk.
